Objectives
===========


PYLEECANâ€™s  objective  is to  provide  a user-friendly, unified, flexible simulation framework for the multiphysic design and optimization of electrical machines and drives based on open-source software. It is meant to be used by researchers, R&D engineers and teachers in electrical engineering, both on standard topologies of electrical machines and on novel topologies (e.g. during a PhD work). An objective of PYLEECAN is that every PhD student should start with PYLEECAN instead of implementing his own routines (e.g. coupling Scilab and Femm).


Features
========

PYLEECAN will include all the following key features to fulfill its objectives:

+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
|     Features                                                            |     PYLEECAN 1.0        |
+=========================================================================+=========================+
| Python-based open-source software                                       |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Graphical User Interface (GUI)                                          |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Object-oriented modeling of electrical machines and drives              |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Multi-physic simulation of electrical machines                          | Yes (electric, magnetic, and force modules) |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Different levels of modeling accuracy (e.g. analytic and numeric)       |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Multi-objective optimization loops including parallelization            |         Yes (MOO)       |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Output data visualization scripts                                       |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
| Online detailed documentation                                           |         Yes             |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------+
